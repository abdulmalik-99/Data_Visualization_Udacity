Investigation Overview¶

In this exploration, I am trying to identify the most important featurse that are related to the Ford Bike Sharing system, and this data covers the Greater San Francisco Bay Area in February 2019. the key insights are:

  • the most of users are subscribers

  • Friday and Thursday less than other day in terms of users

  • The Most trips occur during the working hours of 6am to 9am and 4pm to 6pm

Dataset Overview¶

Ford GoBike is a bike-sharing system in the San Francisco Bay Area, operated by Motivate International. The system allows users to rent bicycles for short trips, using a network of automated kiosks and mobile app. Users can purchase a membership or pay for each ride individually. The system provides data on bike availability, usage, and trip information to the public, which can be used for transportation planning, research, and other purposes.
